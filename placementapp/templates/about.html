About Our Project

Our Placement Prediction System leverages machine learning to analyze student profiles and predict placement opportunities with high accuracy. The system considers academic records, skill sets, certifications, and extracurricular involvement to help students understand their chances in the job market.

This platform is designed to bridge the gap between students and recruiters by offering insightful analytics. It aids career counselors and training departments in identifying at-risk students and enhancing employability strategies.
